How to Test the Flow

  1. Start with Scanning: Go to the Scan page and click "Scan Receipt" to simulate scanning a receipt
  2. Review & Submit: The app will show a preview of the parsed receipt. Click "Auto-Submit Expense" to create a request
  3. Track Requests: Go to Requests to see your submitted expense requests with different statuses
  4. View Details: Click on any request to see detailed information, receipt preview, and timeline
  5. Check Notifications: Visit the Notifications page to see real-time updates about your requests
  6. Manage Expenses: Use the Office page to view categorized expenses and monthly summaries
